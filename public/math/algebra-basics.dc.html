// KEEP IT BALANCED — LEVEL L2 · NUMBER & ALGEBRA

An equation is a balanced scale

The equals sign means both sides weigh exactly the same. Solving for x is just tidying up — and the one golden rule is: whatever you do to one side, you must do to the other, or the scale tips. Solve equations on a real balance below, then feed numbers through a function machine.

// THE GOLDEN RULE
To undo what's happening to x, do the inverse operation — and do it to both sides. Undo + with −, × with ÷. Work in reverse order: peel off the +/− first, then the ×/÷, like taking off shoes before socks.
// CHECK YOUR ANSWER
Always substitute your answer back into the original equation. If x = 3 turns 3x + 2 into 3(3) + 2 = 11 ✓, you're right. This one habit catches almost every slip.
