THE FOUR TRANSFORMATIONS
Reflect flips across a mirror line. Rotate spins about a centre point by an angle. Translate slides by a vector, no turning. All three keep the shape exactly the same size — congruent.
Enlarge is different: a scale factor stretches every length from a centre point. SF > 1 grows it, 0 < SF < 1 shrinks it, and negative SF flips it through the centre too. The result is similar — same angles and shape, different size.
