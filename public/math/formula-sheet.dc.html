// THE TRAP
The most common revision mistake is memorising formulas you'd be given anyway — and skipping the ones you wouldn't. The exam never says "use the cosine rule": the real skill is recognising which formula a question wants. That's what each topic page trains.
// PRACTISE WITH THE REAL SHEET
Print your board's official sheet and keep it beside you for every past paper, so on exam day you know its layout by heart. Watch for board quirks: the sheet gives the cosine rule for finding a side — finding an angle means rearranging it yourself.
