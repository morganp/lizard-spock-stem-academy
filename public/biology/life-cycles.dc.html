Complete metamorphosis
The young looks nothing like the adult and changes completely inside a case. A caterpillar becomes a butterfly; a tadpole becomes a frog. Four very different stages.
Growing up gradually
Other young are just small versions of their parents and slowly get bigger — a chick into a hen, a seed into a plant. Same body plan the whole way through.
