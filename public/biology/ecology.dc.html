Populations rise and fall together

Notice the foxes' peak always comes just after the rabbits' peak: more rabbits → foxes have lots to eat → foxes increase → rabbits get eaten → rabbits crash → foxes starve → rabbits recover. The two cycles are locked together, slightly out of step.
