// 01 — WHY DON'T POLAR BEARS LIVE IN THE DESERT?
Because a thick fur coat that saves your life in the Arctic would cook you in the desert. An adaptation is only useful in the habitat it fits. Move the animal and its survival kit becomes useless — which is exactly why each habitat has its own special cast of creatures, and why protecting habitats matters so much.
