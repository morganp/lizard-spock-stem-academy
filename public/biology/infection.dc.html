// PATHOGENS & DEFENCE — LEVEL L3

Diseases spread — unless enough people are protected

Pathogens are microbes that cause disease. Your body fights them; vaccines train that fight in advance. Set how many people are vaccinated, release the outbreak, and watch herd immunity appear — the disease can't reach the vulnerable if it keeps hitting protected people.
