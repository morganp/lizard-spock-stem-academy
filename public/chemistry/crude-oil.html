Crude oil & polymers

Crude oil is a mixture of hydrocarbon chains of every length — the fossil remains of tiny sea creatures. Split it into useful fractions by boiling point, crack the big ones into petrol and reactive alkenes, then chain those alkenes into the plastics all around you.
