// L3 — WHY THESE TESTS WORK
Flame colours come from electrons in the metal ion absorbing the flame's heat, jumping up an energy level, then falling back and releasing that exact energy as a specific colour of light — the same photon-jump idea from electron shells, just triggered by heat instead of electricity.
